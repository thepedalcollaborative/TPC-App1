Should my compressor go before or after my overdrive?
✦ TPC Advisor

Before, almost always. Before drive = tighter, more even signal. After drive = squashes the character you just created.

What about with fuzz?
✦ TPC Advisor

Fuzz is the exception — it's picky about impedance. Compressor after fuzz, or skip it. Fuzz loves to breathe.
