What's missing from my board for a David Gilmour sound?
✦
Based on your vault, you've already got solid reverb (BigSky MX) and compression (Keeley Compressor Plus) — two pillars of the Gilmour sound. What's missing is a smooth, touch-sensitive fuzz like the Electro-Harmonix Ram's Head Big Muff, and a long, warm slapback delay — your current setup doesn't include a dedicated delay pedal. A Strymon Timeline or even a simpler EHX Memory Man would get you there.
Any budget option for the delay?
✦
For under $150, the MXR Carbon Copy Analog Delay ($129) is hard to beat for a warm Gilmour-adjacent sound. It's also one of the most popular picks in the TPC community right now — lots of players pairing it with the BigSky specifically.
